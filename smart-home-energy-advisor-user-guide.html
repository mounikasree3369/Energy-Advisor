⚡ Smart Home Energy Advisor — User Guide

Everything a citizen needs to know to get value from this app

🌐 Step 1 — Open the App

Make sure the app is running, then open any browser and go to:

http://localhost:5000

You'll land on the Dashboard with your home's energy data already loaded.

📌 Step 2 — Understand the 4 Tabs

📊
Dashboard
Overview of your home — monthly usage, bill, carbon footprint, efficiency score, charts, and energy goals.
🤖
AI Advisor (Chat)
Ask Aria anything about your energy. Get personalized advice powered by IBM Granite AI.
🔌
Appliances
See every appliance ranked by energy use. Filter by category. See cost, CO₂, and efficiency grade per device.
📈
Analytics
Annual bill projection, time-of-use breakdown, carbon summary, and a rate calculator to model your costs.

📊 Tab 1 — Dashboard

💡
4 KPI Cards (top row)
Monthly kWh usage, estimated bill, carbon footprint (kg CO₂), and efficiency score. Green = good, red = needs attention.
📈
12-Month Trend Chart
Bar chart shows monthly kWh usage. Orange line shows estimated cost. Hover over any bar for exact numbers.
🍩
Category Donut Chart
Shows what % of your total energy each category uses — Heating & Cooling, Transportation, Kitchen, etc.
⏰
Hourly Profile Chart
Shows when during the day your home uses the most energy. Red shading = on-peak (expensive) hours (2–9 PM).
⚡
Peak Savings Panel
Shows your on-peak/off-peak rates and how much you could save by shifting usage to cheaper hours.
🎯
Energy Goals
Progress bars showing how close you are to your monthly kWh, cost, and CO₂ reduction targets.

🤖 Tab 2 — AI Advisor (Most Important!)

Click "AI Advisor" in the navigation. Type your question and press Enter or click the send button.

💬 What to ask Aria:

  • 💰"What is my estimated electricity bill this month?"
  • 🔌"Which appliances use the most energy in my home?"
  • ⏰"When is the cheapest time to run my washing machine?"
  • 🌱"How can I reduce my carbon footprint?"
  • ☀️"Should I install solar panels? Will it save money?"
  • 💡"Give me your top 5 energy saving tips"
  • 🏆"What is my home energy efficiency score?"
  • 📉"How much can I save if I shift EV charging to night?"
  • 🌡️"What temperature should I set my thermostat to?"
  • 🔧"My water heater is old — should I replace it?"
💡 Quick prompts: The chat has pre-built buttons like "💰 Estimate my bill", "🌱 Reduce CO₂", "⏰ Peak/off-peak tips" — just click them for instant answers!

🌙 Dark Mode

Click the moon icon (🌙) in the top-right navbar to switch to dark mode. Your preference is saved automatically.

✅ Recommended Workflow for Citizens

  1. Open the Dashboard — check your 4 KPI cards to understand your current situation
  2. Look at the Hourly Chart — see if you use a lot of energy during expensive on-peak hours (2–9 PM red zone)
  3. Go to Appliances — find your top 3 energy consumers and note any C/D/F graded devices
  4. Go to AI Advisor — ask "Give me your top 5 energy saving tips" for personalized advice
  5. Ask Aria about your biggest appliance — e.g. "Should I replace my old water heater?"
  6. Go to Analytics → Rate Calculator — enter your real electricity rate to get accurate cost projections
  7. Come back weekly — track your progress on the Goals progress bars
